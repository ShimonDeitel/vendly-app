Privacy Policy

Vendly is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in this app.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed with it.
